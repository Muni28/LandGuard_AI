The Problem

Land Verification is Broken

Property buyers in India must navigate multiple fragmented government portals just to verify one piece of land — leading to confusion, fraud, and financial loss.

🗂️

Fragmented Data

Land records, registration, and court cases are spread across TNREGINET, CLIP, STAR 3.0, and more — making verification complex and time-consuming.

⚠️

Risk of Fraud

Buyers unknowingly purchase disputed properties due to lack of clear, unified information — resulting in financial loss and legal complications.

🌐

Language Barriers

Complex legal documents in English exclude rural citizens and first-time buyers who need guidance in their native language.

🔔

No Real-Time Alerts

Citizens receive no notification when a legal claim is filed on their property — leaving them unaware until serious damage is done.

The Solution

Everything You Need,
In One Platform

🤖

AI Litigation Risk Score

Our Random Forest & Naive Bayes models analyze historical dispute data to generate a Low / Medium / High risk score for any property instantly.

🔗

Unified Portal Integration

Integrates TNREGINET, CLIP, STAR 3.0, Bhu-Aadhaar/ULPIN, and DILRMP into a single seamless dashboard.

🔔

Real-Time Dispute Alerts

Receive instant notifications the moment any new legal claim or dispute is registered against your property.

🗺️

Visual Boundary Verification

SVAMITVA/NAKSHA-powered drone mapping allows you to visually confirm exact land boundaries before purchase.

🌐

Multilingual Interface

Fully available in Tamil and English — making land verification accessible to all citizens regardless of literacy level.
